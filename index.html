Information

We have started this tutoring service in order to provide help for students struggling in their French classes. Our mission is to help students by offering high-quality, comprehensive and effective services in French. By using lessons and worksheets to allow practice in speaking, listening, reading, and writing, students can gain an understanding of material. We focus on teaching in a way that fits what each student needs. For French 1 and 2 students, our classes are on the weekend and last for 1.5 hours to make sure students get enough time to learn the material. For French 3 and 4 students, our classes are on a flexible schedule to match a busy high school schedule. Students sign up for individual classes based on the time that works for them, there is no fixed schedule or repeated scheduling (yet!).

FAQS

  • Q: What if I can't make it to your classes but I still need help?
    A: That's fine, just email one of us and we can try to come up with a time that works for both of us.
  • Q: When are classes?
    A: Classes will generally be on Saturdays for French 1 and 2. There is a flexible schedule for French 3 and 4 students.
  • Q: How long do I have to sign up?
    A: Sign up forms are due at least 24 hours before the class takes place, service agreement is due at least 2 hours before the class.
  • Q: Where will classes take place?
    A: Classes will typically be online using Zoom.
  • Q: What is the cancellation policy?
    A: If you need to cancel, please do so at least 12 hours before the class. If you cancel within 12 hours, you may be barred future participation.

Once you've signed up, all information will be sent out regarding time and place. If there are any further questions, please contact us or ask our AI agent.
